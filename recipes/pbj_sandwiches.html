Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwiches

Description

A timeless comfort food, the peanut butter and jelly sandwich combines creamy richness with fruity sweetness. Whole wheat bread adds texture and heartiness, while smooth peanut butter and glossy berry jelly create a satisfying contrast. Sliced diagonally for visual appeal, it’s perfect for lunchboxes, quick snacks, or nostalgic cravings. The balance of protein and sugar makes it both energising and indulgent. Simple to make, endlessly customisable, and always a crowd-pleaser—this sandwich never goes out of style.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices whole wheat bread
  • 2 tablespoons smooth peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons berry jelly or jam (e.g. strawberry or raspberry)
  • Optional: butter for toasting

Recipe

  1. Spread peanut butter on one slice of bread and jelly on the other.
  2. Press the slices together, filling sides inward.
  3. Slice in half and serve: untoasted or pan-toasted with butter, your call.

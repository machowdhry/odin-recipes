Cucumber Sandwiches

Description

Cucumber sandwiches are the epitome of refined simplicity. Soft white bread, lightly buttered or spread with cream cheese, cradles thin slices of crisp cucumber. Served chilled and cut into neat triangles, they offer a refreshing bite with subtle flavor and elegant texture. Ideal for afternoon tea or light summer gatherings, these sandwiches are both visually appealing and satisfyingly delicate. Their cool crunch and creamy base make them a timeless classic in British culinary tradition.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices white sandwich bread
  • 4 oz cream cheese or softened butter
  • Â½ cucumber, thinly sliced

Recipe

  1. Spread cream cheese or butter on two slices of bread.
  2. Layer cucumber slices evenly over the spread.
  3. Top with remaining bread, trim crusts, slice into triangles, and serve chilled.
